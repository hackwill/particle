Silicon Edge Robotics

  • Robot Competition

Some people may see me as a solfware only type of guy. But joining the First Tech Challenge team Silicon Edge 10341. Being part of this challenge was one of the best decisions I have made. While on the team, I gained a new perspective of technology and broadened my view and knowledge of coding. The experience I came away with, helping to build and code our robot, was invaluable. Seeing my code producing the commands for our robot, made me more aware of what I was actually coding. Custom IOS app

  • App Development

1/3/2018 The new Mason High School superintendent asked myself and a small group of students to develop a name to face mobile app for IOS, so he could get to know his staff. One of the big issues we came across was that none of us had a Mac. When we realized we needed MacOS to code in Swift and Xcode, we started looking for other was to get this App built. After digging around we found a work around, a program called phonegap, which can take Html, Css,and
Javascript and turns it into a mobile application for IOS.
We are still building the mobile app.
